Workshops by our Team

With never ending passion to explore the boundless limits of robotics, our team also believes that “Sharing Knowledge ensures entry of New”, thus to help students in expanding their knowledge and dive into the world of robotics, our team conducts “Robotics workshop” annually. Where in we teach students, various concepts related to robotics and help them implement the same in their projects.


The topics covered included designing and modelling parts using the CATIA software, study of pneumatic systems, basics of electronics, coding an Arduino and introduction to MATLAB. Each of these sessions were conducted in an interactive manner with the instructors keeping the pace as such so that everyone gets full benefit of the workshop. Each session included a hands-on and demonstration session where students got to implement what they’ve learned and clear any doubts they had. The students loved the hands-on sessions as they allowed them to try out all the ideas they had in their minds. Over 200 students participated in this workshop, this was truly a great learning experience for both the students and the team.

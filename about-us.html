Know More About Us

We are Team Automatons, a group of undergraduate students from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering, Pune. We provide industrial automation solutions and participate in international robotics competition, ABU ROBOCON hosted by Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union, annually.

Our team is devoted towards exploring the various possibilities of the future and believe that only the advancement of science and technology can achieve the true freedom and limitless possibilities of mankind. We constantly strive to explore different technologies and use minimal resources to create efficient robots. At this moment, our team focuses on giving the machine a trace of intelligence through latest mechanical, electrical and control technologies so as to build robots that serve the society besides being an opportunity for students to explore the field of robotics.

Vision

To serve Knowledge and Skills to develop individuals
and teams with the ability to think and get self-reliance.

What we do

Every year we participate in the event ROBOCON organised by Asia - Pacific Broadcasting Union (ABU). Over 20 countries of Asia Pacific region participate in this event. On the National level about 120 colleges participate in this event.
